GDPR COMPLIANCE - DATA RIGHTS
------------------------------
Under GDPR, you have the following rights:
1. Right to Access - You can request access to your personal data
2. Right to Rectification - You can request corrections to your data
3. Right to be Forgotten - You can request deletion of your data
4. Data Portability - You can request your data in a portable format

You may submit a Data Subject Request (DSAR) at any time through our portal.
We will respond within 30 days of receiving your request.

CCPA COMPLIANCE - CALIFORNIA RIGHTS
------------------------------------
California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:
- Right to Opt-Out of the Sale of Personal Information
- Access our "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" page
- Submit a Consumer Request directly or through an Authorized Agent
- No Financial Incentive will be offered for personal data
- Review our Notice at Collection for Categories of Personal Information we collect

PRIVACY BY DESIGN AND DEFAULT
------------------------------
We implement Privacy by Design and Privacy by Default principles throughout
